## Token Allowance

Allowance refers to how many tokens another address is allowed to spend from your wallet.

For example, our [Marketplace](/pre-built-contracts/marketplace) contract asks you permission to **increase your allowance** when you make a bid on an auction listing.

### Get allowance for the connected wallet

Get the number of tokens that another wallet can spend on behalf of the connected wallet.

<CodeSnippetWithSummary
  contract={"TokenDropImpl"}
  name={"allowance"}

/>
